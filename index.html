All Occasions Printables

Bingo Card Generator

Create printable bingo cards from your own list. Paste songs, phrases, names, or prompts, then print at home or save as a PDF.

For personal use only. Your list and uploaded centre image stay in this browser while you create your cards.

Free square

Use simple text, or upload an image/logo for the centre square.

Color scheme

Pick a palette, then fine-tune any color below.

Design options

Adjust the heading, card style, and print layout. The small footer link is added automatically.

If your heading wraps onto two lines, reduce the size slider until it fits.

Printable extras

Add helpful pages for hosting the game. These print after the bingo cards.

Use “Download PDF” for a ready-made file, or “Print or save PDF” if you prefer your browser print window.

Quick tips
  • Use a desktop or laptop for the easiest editing and PDF download.
  • Around 75 items gives good variety when making lots of cards.
  • Shorten very long titles before printing if they look too small.
  • If Download PDF does not work, use Print or save PDF instead.
  • Your settings are saved in this browser. Use Clear saved settings to start again.
